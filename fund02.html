THRIVE MONTHLY GIVING

Join our monthly giving community and help us reach more children in need.
Malnutrition is the single greatest threat to child survival worldwide. For nearly half of the 5.3 million children who don’t live to see their fifth birthday, hunger is a reason why.
We know how to help these children. Hunger is a predictable, preventable, and treatable illness. For more than 40 years, Action Against Hunger has been leading a movement to save lives and improve the health of the world’s most vulnerable children.
THRIVE is a community of monthly donors dedicated to helping us reach more children in need. Monthly donors are with us in 45+ countries, in every major emergency in the world, helping to save lives, empower communities, and ensure more children can grow up strong.

For Action. Against hunger

HOW DOES IT WORK?

  1. Choose an amount. Right now, most people are giving $50/month – that’s the average cost to provide the full course of lifesaving hunger treatment for one child.
  2. Fight hunger with us for one child at a time. THRIVE members make a monthly gift that is automatically transferred from a credit or debit account on the same day each month, and can be adjusted or stopped at any time
  3. Join our lifesaving community. Stay informed and spread the word! As a THRIVE member, you will receive special updates from the field about how your support is helping more children survive and thrive. We’ll also offer you a special welcome gift to help you raise awareness in your community.
